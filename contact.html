About US

We are a youth-led climate initiative established in 2021 with a Seed Grant from Earth Rising Foundation to accelerate youth engagement in climate action in grassroots communities. We offer comprehensive training programs, create diverse opportunities for youth involvement and empowerment, and lead policy and advocacy to ensure that youth voices are heard and integrated into decision-making processes. Our unique approach bridges the gap between climate knowledge and action by providing practical tools and resources for youth to be key actors in community development
