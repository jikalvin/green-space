1 - Climate Leadership and Retreat

Join us and embark on a journey of self-discovery, skill-building, and community engagement.

Our Climate Leadership and Retreat is a unique opportunity and experience for young people to turn their climate anxiety to meaningful action by engaging in a comprehensive leadership development program. During the retreats, Participants will explore key topics such as effective communication, community engagement, project management, and strategic planning focus on addressing climate change.

Leadership Development Participants gain insights into different leadership styles, learn how to effectively lead teams, and develop strategies for driving positive change within their communities.

Environmental Advocacy Our activities focus on empowering participants to become effective advocates for environmental sustainability, equipping them with the knowledge and tools to drive meaningful action.

Self-awareness and Vision Development We help participants build the resilience and skills to managing anxiety and building a positive mind-set in the face of climate change.

Advocacy and Communication Participants develop effective communication strategies and advocacy skills to raise awareness and mobilize support for environmental causes

2 - Youth Climate Boot Camps

Our climate boot camps are design to equip young people with practical skills and knowledge to become climate heroes driving positive change in their communities. During the Boot camp young people engage in hand-on workshops, outdoor exploration and adventures, Environmental games and challenges, gardening, environmental conservation, sustainable cooking, environmental film screening, Art and poetry competitions.

About

Our vibrant and inclusive space is designed to inspire, educate, and empower young people to become catalysts for positive environmental action. Through a diverse range of activities and initiatives, we are committed to nurturing a community of environmentally conscious leaders who are equipped to address pressing climate challenges and foster sustainable practices within their communities and beyond.
