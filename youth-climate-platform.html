Shared Climate Space

Climate activists and youth in grassroots communities have inadequate funds and resources to purchase advocacy materials, secure a location for co-creating, meetings, and trainings. We have created the youth climate space to provide a centralized space for youth climate activist and to provide materials and resources young people need to lead climate change advocacy. But that not all!

We work closely with young climate leaders to understand their vision and unique challenges, spark ideas, build coalitions, and co-create solutions through our monthly mentorship and master classes that build their leadership skills, foster collaboration, encourage networking and resource sharing Book a climate share space for your event or hop in and join our monthly mentorship / masterclass.
