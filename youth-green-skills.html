Cultivating green skills among youth

In an era defined by environmental concerns and the urgent need for sustainable solutions, cultivating green skills among youth is essential. By empowering young people with the knowledge and abilities to understand, protect, and enhance the environment, we are investing in a future where environmental stewardship is a fundamental part of everyday life.

Renewable Energy

Our activities focus on building capacities and empowering young people towards action on the energy transition. We offer a unique, non-formal hand-on disruptive training to build capacities of young people on renewable energy, as well as help them develop and implement clean energy solutions.

Green Agricultural Solutions

We help young people understand where they food comes from as we engage and provide practical hands-on training on sustainable agricultural practices such as regenerative community gardening, organic urban gardening, and climate-smart agricultural technology solutions.

Waste Management and Recycling

We work with young people to transform households waste into cash. We provide practical training that help young people develop solutions to waste challenges in their communities from waste collection, recycling to waste disposal.
