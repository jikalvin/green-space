Our Impact

Projects and updates from Green Space Academy

YOUTH CLIMATE ACCELERATOR

Youth Climate Accelerator is a six-month program that seeks to inspire, prepare, and mobilize the next generation of African climate leaders who provide sustainable solutions to the climate problems facing Africa and our planet through holistic training and mentorship. The Youth Climate accelerator empowers and expands the role of African youths in climate action

YOUTH GRASSROOTS CLIMATE ACTION

Youths in grassroots communities across Sub-Saharan Africa are among the most vulnerable to climate change, bearing the brunt of the crisis. Despite this, over 70% of youths in these communities are unaware of the dangers posed by climate change, and they are regularly underrepresented and excluded from global dialogue

ECO-SCHOOL GARDENS

Many young people are completely disconnected from nature and unaware of where their food is grown. Green Space Academy provides students with the opportunity to learn how to grow food in ways that fight climate change and regenerate our earth.
