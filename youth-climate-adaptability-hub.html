How the Hub Works

Multiple pathways to engage, learn, and lead climate solutions

Training & Workshops

Practical sessions in conservation, agriculture, clean energy, and green entrepreneurship. Our hands-on approach ensures participants gain real-world skills they can apply immediately in their communities.

Innovation Labs

Collaborative spaces where young people experiment with new technologies, develop prototypes, and test solutions to local climate challenges.

Community Projects

Real-world implementation opportunities where participants lead climate adaptation projects in their own communities, supported by mentorship and resources.

Shared Climate Space

Co-creating Climate Solutions Together

Climate activists and youth in grassroots communities have inadequate funds and resources to purchase advocacy materials, secure a location for co-creating, meetings, and trainings. We have created the youth climate space to provide a centralized space for youth climate activists and to provide materials and resources young people need to lead climate change advocacy.

We work closely with young climate leaders to understand their vision and unique challenges, spark ideas, build coalitions, and co-create solutions through our monthly mentorship and master classes that build their leadership skills, foster collaboration, encourage networking and resource sharing.
